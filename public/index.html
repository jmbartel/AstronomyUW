Madison Astronomical Society

Description:

MAS consists of people from many walks of life who are united by a genuine interest in astronomy. Our members are amateurs with skill levels ranging from novice to very experienced. MAS is a non-profit organization promoting observational astronomy and discussions of astronomical topics. Our meetings are held the second Friday of each month usually at UW Space Place, located at 2300 S. Park Street in Madison. Meetings often include discussions as well as speakers on a wide variety of astronomical topics.

Link: https://madisonastro.org/
